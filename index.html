About Founder and Principal

Dr. Rajesh Kumar Sharma

Founder & Principal

Dr. Rajesh Kumar Sharma is an eminent educationist with over 30 years of experience in the field of education. His vision of creating an institution that nurtures young minds while preserving cultural values led to the establishment of Vidya Vihar School. Under his dynamic leadership, the school has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

A gold medalist in Education from Delhi University, Dr. Sharma believes in the philosophy of "Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." His innovative teaching methodologies and student-centric approach have inspired thousands of students to achieve their full potential.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible citizens. We strive to create a nurturing environment where every child discovers their unique potential and develops into a well-rounded individual capable of making positive contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that sets benchmarks in academic excellence, character development, and innovation. We envision creating global citizens who are intellectually curious, culturally rooted, ethically strong, and equipped to face the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and compassion.

The Trust

About Vidya Vihar Trust

Committed to educational excellence and social responsibility

Vidya Vihar Trust is a registered educational trust established with the noble mission of spreading quality education and creating opportunities for holistic development of children. The trust is governed by a dedicated team of educationists, social workers, and community leaders who share a common vision of transforming society through education.

The trust operates with complete transparency, accountability, and adherence to all regulatory guidelines. Our commitment extends beyond academics to include social welfare initiatives, scholarships for deserving students, and community development programs.

Admission Guidelines

1

Registration

Parents can register by visiting the school office during working hours (9:00 AM - 2:00 PM) or by downloading the registration form from our website.

2

Required Documents

Birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), transfer certificate, Aadhar card of student and parents, passport size photographs (4 copies), and residential proof.

3

Interaction

An informal interaction will be conducted with the child and parents to understand the student's capabilities and interests.

4

Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates will be notified within 7 working days. Admission must be confirmed by paying the required fees within the stipulated time.

5

Age Criteria

For Pre-Nursery: 2.5+ years, Nursery: 3+ years, KG-1: 4+ years, KG-2: 5+ years, Class 1: 6+ years as on 31st March of the admission year.
